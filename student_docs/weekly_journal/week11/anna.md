## Design decisions
- Fixed the resizing on the CA Diagram to better adapt to different screen sizes.
- Added a sidebar to the CA Diagram pages to hold the learning mode descriptions on learning mode page, or the issues on the use case interaction pages.
- Decided to move the learning mode page descriptions to the side bar so the font size could be bigger, and it would be faster to implement.
- Within this side panel, made sure the chevron that opens and closes the menu was visible in both open and closed states by placing it in a gutter to the left of the side bag with a transparent background.
- Straightened out the arrows coming out of the frameworks and drivers layer.
