# Week 3 Journal - Vivian

## Overview
This week was dedicated to the iterative process of design refinement for our Figma mockup. Building on the feedback received during our meeting in Week 2, I focused on enhancing the usability and clarity of the application. The primary goal was to transform the initial mockup into a more intuitive tool for students, specifically addressing navigation (File Explorer), clarity (Legend), and actionable feedback (Suggestions).

## Learning Objectives Met

### 2. Master high-fidelity prototyping and design tooling
* **Complex Component Creation:** I utilized Figma to design more complex, information-dense components required for the technical nature of the tool.
    * **Legend & Visual Guides:** I implemented a betteer legend system. This involved defining specific colors and arrow types to represent different architectural relationships, ensuring that the visual data is self-explanatory for the user.
    * **Home Page Architecture:** I completely redesigned the home page to serve as a better navigation hub. The new layout features three distinct entry points—**Clean Architecture Checker Mode**, **Learning Mode**, and **Starter Files Mode**—which significantly improves the user flow and helps students immediately find the specific tool they need.

### 4. Implement an iterative stakeholder feedback loop
* **Feedback Integration:** I directly translated the insights gathered from the meeting into specific design tasks. The Professors highlighted areas where the initial design lacked clarity or actionability, and I prioritized these for Week 3.
* **Design Iterations:**
    * **Home Page Redesign:** I overhauled the home page layout based on feedback to improve the information hierarchy and entry point for the user.
    * **Actionable Insights:** To address the need for constructive feedback, I added a "Suggestions" section located immediately below the "Violations" display. This ensures that when a student sees an error, they are immediately presented with the context on how to fix it.
